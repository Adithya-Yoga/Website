About Us

More than a class — a way of living

At Adithya Yoga, we believe every breath is an invitation to come home to yourself. Our studio in Thrissur offers a warm, inclusive space rooted in the classical traditions of Hatha and Vinyasa yoga, thoughtfully adapted for modern practitioners of all levels.

  • Small groups for personalised attention
  • Certified instructor with 5+ years of experience
  • Both beginner-friendly and advanced tracks
  • Flexible morning & evening schedules
  • Meditation and pranayama included
